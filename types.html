Fun Facts

You can be colour blind in one eye, but have completely regular vision in the other eye.


All babies are born colour blind, and develop the ability to see colour as they grow.


The mantis shrimp can see 4x the amount of colours than a human can.


Those with colour blindness can often find it difficult to tell the difference between ripened and non-ripe foods.
